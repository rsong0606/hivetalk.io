What is LinkieTalk

LinkieTalk is an AI-powered language learning app focusing on interactive chats and real-world practice. Key features include:

Demo WalkThrough

Dictionary Lookup

Find word meanings, usage, and pronunciation.

Casual Conversation

Chat with the bot on various topics to improve your skills.

Learn with Pictures

Associate words and phrases with images for better memory.

Daily Phrases

Master common expressions used in everyday life.

Scenario Dialogue

Practice English in simulated real-life situations.

Language Translator

Quickly translate between Chinese and English.

English Test

Assess your language proficiency and identify areas to improve.

Dev-in-progress

Upcoming features include advanced analytics, customizable learning paths, interactive quizzes, and more.

Our Story

We founded LinkieTalk to tackle the real-world challenge of language learning. As non-native speakers, we encountered numerous obstacles in mastering English. Our journey inspired us to create a solution that is both practical and engaging. With the power of AI, LinkieTalk provides personalized, interactive learning experiences tailored to each user's needs.

We believe that learning a new language should be an exciting adventure, not a daunting task. Our mission is to make language learning accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone. Join us on this journey to break language barriers and connect with the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your company creating?

We're developing a language learning app that focuses on interactive chatting and real-world practice. Users enjoy tailored English learning journeys, featuring a dictionary, chatbot-driven conversation practice, visual aids, and more.

Who are your customers?

Our customers are students and non-native English speakers from beginners to advanced learners who need practical English language practice.

What problems do you solve?

  • Language Barriers: Our AI-powered app provides context-based learning to help users apply their skills in daily situations.
  • Personalized Learning: We offer personalized experiences, adjusting to each user's proficiency level.
  • Limited Practice: Our chatbot simulates real-life conversations for enhanced speaking and listening skills.
  • Accessibility: Our freemium model ensures essential tools are free, with affordable upgrades for advanced features.

Why this idea? Do you have expertise?

As non-native speakers, we faced challenges in learning English. Discovering AI's potential, we saw an opportunity for personalized learning experiences. Our team has expertise in data science and technology to bring this vision to life.

What sets you apart?

Unlike others, we offer a complete language toolkit for everyday use, integrating contextual learning. Our agility allows us to quickly respond to feedback and implement new features, providing a more personalized experience.

What's your business model?

We use a freemium subscription model, offering most features for free with upgrades available. We also plan collaborations with educational institutions and in-app purchases for specialized learning packs.
